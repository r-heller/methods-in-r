\section{CONSORT --- randomised trials}\label{consort-randomised-trials}

CONSORT --- \emph{Consolidated Standards of Reporting Trials} --- covers
randomised controlled trials. The 25-item checklist and flow diagram are
at \url{https://www.consort-statement.org/}. The templates below cover
the analytic methods most often used for primary and secondary endpoints
in a trial.

This page contains \textbf{141 method-specific templates}. Each is a
skeleton with placeholders in italics; the prose is written so you can
paste it into a manuscript and replace only the bracketed values.
