APPENDIX · GLOSSARY

\section{Glossary}\label{glossary}

Plain-English definitions of the terms this curriculum uses the most.

Each entry links to the first lab in which the term appears.

\subsection{A}\label{a}

\textbf{α (alpha).} The probability of a type I error --- rejecting a
true null hypothesis. Conventional values are 0.05 and 0.01.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-hypothesis-testing-p-values-type-i-ii-errors.Rmd}{Hypothesis
testing p values type i ii errors}.

\textbf{ANCOVA.} Analysis of covariance: a linear model combining
categorical predictors and continuous covariates, commonly used to
adjust RCT analyses for baseline.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-ancova-in-rcts-baseline-adjustment.Rmd}{Ancova
in rcts baseline adjustment}.

\textbf{ANOVA.} Analysis of variance: a linear model with categorical
predictors.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-one-way-anova-and-contrasts-emmeans.Rmd}{One
way anova and contrasts emmeans}.

\textbf{Assumption.} A condition the chosen test needs to be
interpretable --- normality, equal variance, independence, linearity.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-sample-and-paired-t-tests.Rmd}{Two
sample and paired t tests}.

\subsection{B}\label{b}

\textbf{Bias.} Systematic deviation of an estimator from the quantity
being estimated.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-populations-samples-and-the-central-limit-theorem.Rmd}{Populations
samples and the central limit theorem}.

\textbf{Bootstrap.} Resampling with replacement to approximate the
sampling distribution of a statistic.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-bootstrap-and-permutation-tests.Rmd}{Bootstrap
and permutation tests}.

\textbf{Brier score.} Mean squared error between predicted probabilities
and observed outcomes.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-calibration-discrimination-roc-auc-brier-score.Rmd}{Calibration
discrimination roc auc brier score}.

\subsection{C}\label{c}

\textbf{CI (confidence interval).} A range of plausible values for a
parameter; in repeated sampling, 95\% CIs cover the true value 95\% of
the time.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-bootstrap-and-permutation-tests.Rmd}{Bootstrap
and permutation tests}.

\textbf{Cohen's d.} Standardised difference in means; a common effect
size for two-group comparisons.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-sample-and-paired-t-tests.Rmd}{Two
sample and paired t tests}.

\textbf{Competing risk.} An event whose occurrence precludes or alters
the probability of the event of interest.
\href{../chapters/11-survival/labs/lab-competing-risks-and-multistate-models.Rmd}{Competing
risks and multistate models}.

\textbf{Confounding.} A third variable distorting the association
between exposure and outcome.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-multiple-regression-confounding-interaction-centring.Rmd}{Multiple
regression confounding interaction centring}.

\textbf{Cox model.} A proportional-hazards regression for time-to-event
outcomes.
\href{../chapters/11-survival/labs/lab-survival-primer-km-log-rank-cox-ph.Rmd}{Survival
primer km log rank cox ph}.

\textbf{CV (cross-validation).} Splitting data into folds to estimate
generalisation error.
\href{../chapters/13-ml/labs/lab-cross-validation-nested-cv-bootstrap-632.Rmd}{Cross
validation nested cv bootstrap 632}.

\subsection{D}\label{d}

\textbf{DAG (directed acyclic graph).} A graphical representation of
causal assumptions.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-dags-with-dagitty-and-ggdag.Rmd}{Dags
with dagitty and ggdag}.

\subsection{E}\label{e}

\textbf{Effect size.} A standardised measure of the magnitude of an
effect, independent of sample size.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-sample-and-paired-t-tests.Rmd}{Two
sample and paired t tests}.

\subsection{F}\label{f}

\textbf{FDR (false discovery rate).} The expected proportion of false
positives among rejected nulls.
\href{../chapters/13-ml/labs/lab-fdr-knockoffs-replication-crisis.Rmd}{Fdr
knockoffs replication crisis}.

\textbf{Fisher's exact test.} Exact test of independence for 2×2 tables,
appropriate when expected cell counts are small.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-proportions-chi-square-risk-and-odds.Rmd}{Two
proportions chi square risk and odds}.

\subsection{G}\label{g}

\textbf{GAM (generalised additive model).} A regression with smooth
non-linear terms, fitted via penalised splines.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-gams-with-mgcv.Rmd}{Gams with
mgcv}.

\textbf{GEE (generalised estimating equations).} A marginal-model
approach for clustered or repeated data.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-glmms-and-gee.Rmd}{Glmms and gee}.

\textbf{GLM (generalised linear model).} A regression with a link
function and exponential-family error.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-logistic-regression-binomial-glm.Rmd}{Logistic
regression binomial glm}.

\subsection{H}\label{h}

\textbf{Hazard ratio.} Ratio of hazard rates between two groups in a
survival model.
\href{../chapters/11-survival/labs/lab-survival-primer-km-log-rank-cox-ph.Rmd}{Survival
primer km log rank cox ph}.

\subsection{I}\label{i}

\textbf{ICC (intraclass correlation).} Proportion of variance
attributable to clustering.
\href{../chapters/14-clinical/labs/lab-kappa-icc-bland-altman.Rmd}{Kappa
icc bland altman}.

\textbf{Interaction.} The effect of one predictor depends on the value
of another.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-multiple-regression-confounding-interaction-centring.Rmd}{Multiple
regression confounding interaction centring}.

\textbf{IPTW (inverse-probability-of-treatment weighting).}
Propensity-score method that reweights observations to emulate a trial.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-propensity-scores-and-iptw.Rmd}{Propensity
scores and iptw}.

\subsection{K}\label{k}

\textbf{Kaplan-Meier.} Non-parametric estimator of the survival
function.
\href{../chapters/11-survival/labs/lab-survival-primer-km-log-rank-cox-ph.Rmd}{Survival
primer km log rank cox ph}.

\textbf{Kruskal-Wallis.} Non-parametric one-way ANOVA on ranks.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-non-parametric-tests.Rmd}{Non
parametric tests}.

\subsection{L}\label{l}

\textbf{Lasso.} L1-regularised regression; produces sparse coefficient
estimates.
\href{../chapters/13-ml/labs/lab-regularisation-ridge-lasso-elastic-net.Rmd}{Regularisation
ridge lasso elastic net}.

\textbf{Likelihood.} The probability of the observed data under a model,
viewed as a function of the parameters.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-maximum-likelihood-estimation.Rmd}{Maximum
likelihood estimation}.

\textbf{Linear model.} A model of the form \(y = X\beta + \varepsilon\).
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-simple-linear-regression.Rmd}{Simple
linear regression}.

\textbf{Logistic regression.} GLM with a logit link for binary outcomes.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-logistic-regression-binomial-glm.Rmd}{Logistic
regression binomial glm}.

\subsection{M}\label{m}

\textbf{MAR (missing at random).} Missingness depends on observed
variables only.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-mcar-mar-mnar.Rmd}{Mcar mar mnar}.

\textbf{MCAR (missing completely at random).} Missingness is independent
of all variables.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-mcar-mar-mnar.Rmd}{Mcar mar mnar}.

\textbf{MCMC.} Markov-chain Monte Carlo; the Bayesian posterior-sampling
workhorse.
\href{../chapters/10-bayesian/labs/lab-brms-stan-loo-hierarchical-models.Rmd}{Brms
stan loo hierarchical models}.

\textbf{MDE (minimum detectable effect).} Smallest effect your study has
power to detect.
\href{../chapters/05-power/labs/lab-power-closed-form-and-simulation.Rmd}{Power
closed form and simulation}.

\textbf{Meta-analysis.} Combining effect estimates across studies.
\href{../chapters/15-metaanalysis/labs/lab-meta-analysis-basics.Rmd}{Meta
analysis basics}.

\textbf{Mixed model.} Regression combining fixed and random effects.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-linear-mixed-models-with-lme4.Rmd}{Linear
mixed models with lme4}.

\textbf{MNAR (missing not at random).} Missingness depends on unobserved
values. \href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-mcar-mar-mnar.Rmd}{Mcar mar
mnar}.

\textbf{Multiple imputation.} Imputing missing values several times and
pooling the analyses.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-multiple-imputation-with-mice.Rmd}{Multiple
imputation with mice}.

\subsection{N}\label{n}

\textbf{Non-parametric.} A test or estimator that makes weak
distributional assumptions.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-non-parametric-tests.Rmd}{Non
parametric tests}.

\subsection{O}\label{o}

\textbf{Odds ratio.} Ratio of odds between two groups; the natural scale
for logistic regression.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-logistic-regression-binomial-glm.Rmd}{Logistic
regression binomial glm}.

\textbf{Outlier.} An observation far from the bulk of the data.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-diagnostics-residuals-qq-leverage-cook-s-distance-vif.Rmd}{Diagnostics
residuals qq leverage cook s distance vif}.

\textbf{Overdispersion.} Variance exceeding the model's nominal
variance; common in Poisson regression.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-poisson-and-negative-binomial-regression.Rmd}{Poisson
and negative binomial regression}.

\subsection{P}\label{p}

\textbf{p-value.} Probability of data as or more extreme than observed,
assuming the null.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-hypothesis-testing-p-values-type-i-ii-errors.Rmd}{Hypothesis
testing p values type i ii errors}.

\textbf{Paired test.} A test comparing matched observations rather than
independent samples.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-sample-and-paired-t-tests.Rmd}{Two
sample and paired t tests}.

\textbf{PCA (principal components analysis).} Linear dimension reduction
by orthogonal projection onto directions of maximum variance.
\href{../chapters/08-multivariate/labs/lab-pca-factor-analysis-cca-lda.Rmd}{Pca
factor analysis cca lda}.

\textbf{Permutation test.} Inference by shuffling labels to build a null
distribution.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-bootstrap-and-permutation-tests.Rmd}{Bootstrap
and permutation tests}.

\textbf{Poisson regression.} GLM with a log link for counts.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-poisson-and-negative-binomial-regression.Rmd}{Poisson
and negative binomial regression}.

\textbf{Power.} Probability of detecting an effect if it exists; 1 − β.
\href{../chapters/05-power/labs/lab-sample-size-power-and-quarto-reporting.Rmd}{Sample
size power and quarto reporting}.

\textbf{Pre-registration.} A timestamped record of the research plan
before data analysis.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-pre-registration-and-statistical-analysis-plans.Rmd}{Pre
registration and statistical analysis plans}.

\textbf{Propensity score.} Probability of treatment given covariates.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-propensity-scores-and-iptw.Rmd}{Propensity
scores and iptw}.

\textbf{Pseudoreplication.} Treating correlated observations as
independent replicates.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-bench-and-translational-design.Rmd}{Bench
and translational design}.

\subsection{R}\label{r}

\textbf{Random effect.} A model coefficient treated as a draw from a
distribution.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-linear-mixed-models-with-lme4.Rmd}{Linear
mixed models with lme4}.

\textbf{Randomisation.} Allocating units to arms by a chance mechanism.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-randomised-controlled-trials.Rmd}{Randomised
controlled trials}.

\textbf{Regression to the mean.} Tendency of extreme values to be closer
to the mean on remeasurement.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-dichotomisation-change-scores-regression-to-the-mean.Rmd}{Dichotomisation
change scores regression to the mean}.

\textbf{Reliability.} Consistency of repeated measurements.
\href{../chapters/14-clinical/labs/lab-kappa-icc-bland-altman.Rmd}{Kappa
icc bland altman}.

\textbf{Resampling.} Bootstrap, permutation, and cross-validation
collectively.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-bootstrap-and-permutation-tests.Rmd}{Bootstrap
and permutation tests}.

\textbf{Residual.} Observed minus predicted.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-diagnostics-residuals-qq-leverage-cook-s-distance-vif.Rmd}{Diagnostics
residuals qq leverage cook s distance vif}.

\textbf{Risk ratio.} Ratio of event probabilities between two groups.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-proportions-chi-square-risk-and-odds.Rmd}{Two
proportions chi square risk and odds}.

\textbf{Robust SE.} A standard error computed without assuming
homoscedasticity.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-robust-regression-weighted-ls-hc-sandwich-standard-errors.Rmd}{Robust
regression weighted ls hc sandwich standard errors}.

\textbf{ROC / AUC.} Receiver operating characteristic; area under it
measures discrimination.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-calibration-discrimination-roc-auc-brier-score.Rmd}{Calibration
discrimination roc auc brier score}.

\subsection{S}\label{s}

\textbf{SAP (statistical analysis plan).} The formal plan for a trial's
analysis, written before data are seen.
\href{../chapters/16-design/labs/lab-pre-registration-and-statistical-analysis-plans.Rmd}{Pre
registration and statistical analysis plans}.

\textbf{SE (standard error).} Standard deviation of a sampling
distribution.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-populations-samples-and-the-central-limit-theorem.Rmd}{Populations
samples and the central limit theorem}.

\textbf{SEM (standard error of the mean).} Standard error of a sample
mean.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-populations-samples-and-the-central-limit-theorem.Rmd}{Populations
samples and the central limit theorem}.

\textbf{Shapiro-Wilk.} A test of normality, powerful in small samples.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-two-sample-and-paired-t-tests.Rmd}{Two
sample and paired t tests}.

\textbf{Spearman correlation.} Rank-based correlation.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-pearson-and-spearman-correlation.Rmd}{Pearson
and spearman correlation}.

\textbf{Survival.} Time-to-event analysis.
\href{../chapters/11-survival/labs/lab-survival-primer-km-log-rank-cox-ph.Rmd}{Survival
primer km log rank cox ph}.

\subsection{T}\label{t}

\textbf{Type I / II error.} False-positive and false-negative errors of
a test.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-hypothesis-testing-p-values-type-i-ii-errors.Rmd}{Hypothesis
testing p values type i ii errors}.

\subsection{V}\label{v}

\textbf{VIF (variance inflation factor).} Measure of collinearity among
regression predictors.
\href{../chapters/07-regression/labs/lab-diagnostics-residuals-qq-leverage-cook-s-distance-vif.Rmd}{Diagnostics
residuals qq leverage cook s distance vif}.

\subsection{W}\label{w}

\textbf{Wilcoxon test.} Non-parametric test for paired (signed-rank) or
unpaired (rank-sum) data.
\href{../chapters/04-inferential/labs/lab-non-parametric-tests.Rmd}{Non
parametric tests}.
